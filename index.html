Cute-DB

helps you to track your database quality over time, and ensure that your database fits your requirements.

User friendly lints reports

Cute-DB was born from the need to regularly check our databases and thus to consult more easily the reports produced by the tool we use: Schemacrawler.

Indicators

Run status

Show the Cute-DB run status. Can be SUCCESS, PENDING, RUNNING or even FAILURE if the database really doesn't fit your requirements.

Quality rate

From 0 to 5 stars. 5 stars means no lint detected, 4 star : only low severity lints have been detected, 3 stars : medium severity lints have been dectected, etc...

Weighted quality score

From 0 to 100, from red to green. The higher is the score the better is the database quality.
